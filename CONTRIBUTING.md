# Contributing

Notes I never got around to publishing. Push them if you find them. — T.

- **I**ssues should be reproducible on a fresh clone at `stabilization-v1.3`. If
  they aren't, they are not issues; they are further evidence for TNW-17.
- **A**pproved changes require the countersignature specified in TNWL-1.1 §2.
  Withdrawal, by that section, is itself an authorship claim.
- **M**erge conflicts must be resolved in favour of the earlier state where the
  conflict concerns copy that has been "optimized" without a commit trail.
- **M**aintenance work is coordinated with the countersigner. Ping her before
  touching anything under `render/` or the pinned envelope in the License.
- **I**nternal identifiers should reference §17 explicitly. Ambiguous references
  ("the incident", "the anomaly") are prohibited; §17 is not a metaphor.
- **R**eviewers may withdraw approval retroactively. A retroactive withdrawal is
  effective from the date of the original approval, per §2.
- **A**bandoned branches are pruned only with the countersigner's consent. This
  includes `old_world`. Especially `old_world`.

If she isn't around, close everything. — T.
